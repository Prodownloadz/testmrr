What is Personal Injury Litigation?

In order the result of negligence or wrong action of others intentionally/non-intentionally, if a person is being injured. It leads damages physically as well as psychologically.

Dealing with personal injury cases

Every claim is unique. The situations and circumstances in a real life of a plaintiff is different. We are not the team who do the service only on what attorneys come for. Beyond that, we are a proper assessor of the life impact cases and caretaker of our clients. We aim to establish a good relationship with attorneys through excellent listeners on the demand of cases.

What are the types of damages existing in Personal Injury?

  • Medical expenses
  • Loss of earning capacity
  • Loss of household services
  • Wrongful death damages
  • Punitive damages
  • Physical pain
  • Mental Anguish
  • Disfigurement
  • Physical impairment
  • Loss of consortium
  • Attorney's fees
  • Costs of court

We are having quite a lot of experience in personal injury cases as received numerous claims every day.

Personal Injury Litigation arise from the cases such as

Motor Vehicle Accidents

The motor vehicle accidents are becoming a serious threat to the public because of the diverted attentions by means of engaging in the activities such as using cell phone, drunk, talking with co-passengers, hearing radio/songs. The claim will be lodged on negligent driver by focusing the compensation of Medical expenses, Rehabilitation expenses, Pain and Suffering expenses, Loss of earning capacity and Attendant care expenses.

Aviation Accidents

Even if the aviation industry has high standard of safety management, actual factors associated with an aviation accident is literally thousand. There are thousands of ways existing for crashes happening in an aircraft based upon underlying situational, demographic and cognitive mechanisms of accident causation. MRR ensures the expertise of claim management on aviation accident litigation with the complete physical and psychological care.

Product liability

If harmful consequences occurred because of the usage of product with defects, it is product liability allegation. In this case, plaintiff is required to prove a defect in the product. The types of defect are Manufacturing defects, Design defects and Warning defects. Collecting evidences of all the sources of defect is a main picture of the product liability claim. We have a team of expert doctors and physicians who have decades of experience in handling this type of claims.

Workplace injury

In course of the action done unsafely in the workplace environment or any job is done in the scope of employment, if the injury occurs, the liability is in the end of employer. A lawsuit against the employer can be filed for occupational disease also. In this kind of cases, there is limited amount of time to submit the paperwork. The reporting injuries should be written in a high focus as it needs to be evaluated with the state law compliances.

Tourist Injury

Tourist injuries are happened for the tourists as unfortunate incidents like taxi cab accidents, theme park accidents, assaults, drowning, etc., on their happy trip. It is imagined as typically a difficult claim to be handled by the plaintiff as the process is not feasible on the defendant’s location. But, that’s not true. The distance should not be reason for the justice what you should deserve and compensation you must get.

Students Injury

Unless the thought of parents as schools and colleges are very safe, the reality is possibility of injuries is lot in schools and college also. The Personal Injury may happen due to the trips or falls caused in result of unmaintained school playgrounds, floors and labs. For proper investigation on this case, the notice of the claim must be in writing format. In this claim, our doctors team clearly describe the nature of incident, details of injuries and demand of compensation.

Burn Injury

Most commonly burn injuries are happened due to fires at public place and residential places, accidents, workplace injuries, electrical accidents, etc., The claiming capability is depends on the various unique factors according to the situation. For a plaintiff, it is considerably for moving on the steps for the legal claiming process. MRR Team is having an excellent and caring perception on dealing with the claim of Burn Injuries. We know how the burn injuries are life changing incident for a people.

Wrongful death

Wrongful Death is happening frequently because of medical malpractice, accidents, slip and fall, etc., If our loved one is lost because of someone carelessness, then, the family is in a strong need of getting justice in that. This kind of summaries always playing a vital role in the trial.It involves economic and non-economic damages. Economic damages like loss of income, medical expenses are compensable. But the non-economic damages such as loss of support and affection is non compensable actually. We are giving our utmost care on this claim.

Other Personal injury cases we handled apart from all above are:

  • Pedestrian accidents
  • Dog bite
  • Birth Injuries
  • Psychological Trauma

In handling of personal injury cases, every perception of details preparation is to be set with three purposes. They are:

  • The first is to compensate plaintiffs who are injured by a defendant’s conduct.
  • The second is to deter persons from acting in ways that may cause injury to others.
  • A third purpose - albeit one of somewhat lesser significance - is to punish people who wrongfully injure others.
