How to present a medico-legal case in detailed timeline?

Answer should be “Medical Chronology Report”!

Medical Chronology Report is a crucial source for an attorney which makes the medico-legal case to be presented every bit of inputs with the arrangements of events or dates in order of the occurrence.

It is a chart of medical history having the importance of ensuring the completeness in records and accuracy at every instant of information as well as timeline.

MRR, we are the team of experts will always get in to the chronological work post complete understanding of

  • Health Care Provider
  • Patient’s Health
  • Treatment taken
  • Possible Issues with the Patient
  • Consequences of events and incident

The medical chronological report prepared by MRR is always ensure inclusion of important facts. The effective chronological report is playing a vital role in the success of a medico-legal cases.

Understands why Medical Chronology is needed in an action instead of Medical Summary

Chronology deals with the subject, object, assessment and plan with the date information of medical records but the summary is going to be a paragraph form regardless of timeline.

What works best in our Medical Chronological Report?

  • Indexing of the details for easy locating of details
  • Not including the information that will nothing to do with the case
  • Not including the opinions or interpretation of medical records
  • Encompassing each relevant piece of details
  • Highlighting the details which witness the case win
  • Accurate reporting of records, notes, treatment, dates, etc.,
  • Hyperlinking for easy navigation with multiple documents
  • Avoiding duplication with the details
