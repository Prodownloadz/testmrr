Narrative Summary

Is it challenging of Consicing the bundle of records in a medico legal case?

Overcome the challenge with “Medical Narrative Summary”

Medical Narrative Summary is providing a summarized report of the patient’s course of medical events which states the facts in a narrative format with chronological order.

It is facilitating attorneys to evaluate a case easily in terms of focusing the prioritized aspects of allegations in a running or completed medico legal trial.

The method of writing narrative summary for a medico legal completed trial and running is different based on the objective of the case.

Composition of a Medical Narrative Summary

  • Patient’s profile
  • Patient’s medical event history in a chronological flow
  • Prior or Present Medication or Medical Conditions
  • Overview of the medical events
  • Overview of the result of medical tests carried out
  • Listing of Treatments taken

Understands why Medical Narrative Summary is needed in an action instead of Medical Chronology

Medical Narrative Summary is concentrating in the comprehensive analysis of entire medical events and providing the brief summary but Medical Chronology is giving a care of arranging the detailed medical events in terms of timeline.

What works best in our Medical Narrative Summary?

  • Careful review of data sources in prior of writing
  • Achieving high consistency by means of Quality Control
  • Pictorial presentation of summary using graph and bar chart
  • Quick customizable format in the need of change
  • Maintain audit trail and record tracking
  • On time Narrative summary submission
  • Understands case and relevancy highlighting
  • Proper cross-referencing the summary with record

How Medical Narrative Summary aid attorneys in Medico Legal Trial?

Show an organized summary from thousands of unorganized records

Provide instant access of navigation to the required records through hyperlink

Interpreting the complex medical terminology in to a common understandable format

Interpreting the complex medical abbreviations and symbol in to a common understandable format

Focusing the relevant facts of a medico legal trial

Saving time by avoiding the situation of getting in to the volumized records.

The inconsistency factors to be avoided in Medical Narrative Summary are:

  • Missing important medical events
  • Missing of supporting reports
  • Confusing the objective of narrative summary
