Medical Chart

A medical chart is a valuable clinical and legal document. It contains records of every medical event such as disease, injuries, test, diagnosis and so on.

It is a crucial move in the life cycle of Personal Injury, Medical Malpractice and Worker’s Compensation Lawsuit. It aids in assessing the claim's legitimacy.

It gives a detailed account of all that has happened to the patient historically that acts as a vital source of trial. It aids in the diagnosis of the present state of health and future care.

Different types of medical charts we offer

Treatment Chart

Purpose:

Reporting the detailed medical information about the patient's injuries, treatment, resulting pain and suffering.

Contains:

In a spreadsheet, the pain symptoms, diagnoses, and treatments for the various diagnoses are presented. It can be customized based on the required specifications.

Sample of Treatment Chart

Comparative Chart

Purpose:

Differentiating the Diagnosis, Medical Conditions, Injuries, Tests before and after the treatment.

For ease of understanding and cross-reference, the important information before and after the collision were provided in a tabular format.

Contains:

They are the list of associated diagnoses, medical studies, claimant's functional stage, and care obtained prior to and after the injury

Sample of Comparative Chart

Accident Timeline

Purpose:

Reporting the happenings of the accident event in a clear timeline format. A chronological overview of the accident’s claim and personal injury case from the initial accident state to the final settlement and litigation.

Contains:

Date, Time, Accident Cause, Person Involved, Injuries, Evidence data, Treatment, etc.,

Sample of Accident Timeline

Pain Score Chart

Purpose:

A pain score chart is a table that depicts various degrees of pain, ranging from mild to extreme denoting in the scoring values for understanding.

Contains:

Date, Pain Area, Pain Score, Reference details and so on.

Sample of Pain Score Chart

List of Injuries

Purpose:

Reporting of injuries for having analysis of the treatment plan in detail against every injury. It provides the chronological list for injuries with the relevant important details.

Contains:

Date, Injuries, Area of Injury, Treatment Plan and so on.

Sample of List of Injuries

Pain and Suffering Chart

Purpose:

Evaluating the pain and the suffering incurred in an incident for a claimant.

It’s a table which is also includes physical therapy interventions and their outcomes, pain consultations, procedures, and their development.

Contains:

Date, provider, main complaints, and position of pain, pain medications, scale of pain, prognosis, assessment, and PDF/Bates reference.

Sample of Pain and Suffering Chart

Pain and Medication Graph

Purpose:

It is a chart in graphical representation that depicts the type of medical care provided to a patient, as well as the amount of pain and discomfort they have suffered.

Also, aid an attorney in determining if the patient has been injured as a result of a pain medication overdose.

Contains:

Alleviate pain, inflammation, or other injury symptoms may help persuade the defendant or insurance adjuster that the injuries are severe.

Sample of Pain and Medication Graph

Diagnostics Chart

Purpose:

Detailed diagnostics study of the injury and the cause. Assess a disorder, disease, or illness by identifying an individual's particular areas of weakness relevant to the injuries suffered at the time of the loss.

Contains:

Date, Study, Provider, Indication, Findings, Impression, More details.

Sample of Diagnostics Chart
