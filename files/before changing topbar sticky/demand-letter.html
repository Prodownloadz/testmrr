Demand Letter

90% of the cases are settled by way of demand letter. What is Demand Letter?

Demand letter is a report containing all the relevant information of your medico-legal case that tends to do summarization of your claims which includes injuries, property damage, wage loss and emotional trauma.

Our medico legal specialist physicians are usually involving in the preparation settlement demand letter. They will analyze the case files, medical records thoroughly, understand the social context of plaintiff in prior of writing a detailed demand letter. The demand letter will be written with the cases such as curing a breach, demanding compensation, insurance claims that covers personal and property damages, resolution of malpractice action, etc.

Composition of a Demand Letter

  • The strong compliance explanation of opponent
  • Details of damages and the severity of that
  • Financial losses details
  • Timeline of settling the dispute
  • Affecting factors of future life
  • Demand compensation conclusion
