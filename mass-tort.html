What is Mass Tort?

The mass tort more or less is an injury or death caused to lot of people usually through a specific product. A large number of people who have been harmed in a similar way but the injuries may have little difference. Mass tort cases are not class actions that the many people do not understand. For instance, a power plant or a chemical plant explodes. Due to that, a lot of workers are burned, paralyzed, lose arms and legs that’s a mass tort. But each of those workers still has their own individual lawsuit.

It is usually arising in the form of

 

Defective Products

Harmful Medications, Dangerous Medical Devices, Etc.,

Environmental disasters

Oil Spill, Power Outage, Etc.,

Consolidation process in Mass tort

It is trying to gather and compile information that is the same or similar across the broad spectrum of all the plaintiffs involved in the case. When we have thousands of cases pending, it is important to simply try to categorize the cases in order to understand where the similarities and dissimilarities may lie once the plaintiff fact sheet has been received.

Dealing with Mass tort

Selecting the similar cases from the broader pool of litigation for the purpose of understanding the value of the case, the strength and weakness of both the defendants and plaintiffs. It has to be done in an effort to try to hopefully come up with some sort of value of global resolution and the finality of the case in favor of plaintiff.
